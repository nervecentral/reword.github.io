Turn Visitors Into Leads & Leads Into Customers With Direct Response Copywriting

So what does a dead ex Navy commander have to do with making you more sales?

Well, you may have heard of a famous ad with the headline “They Laughed When I Sat Down At the Piano But When I Started to Play!”

John Caples wrote this ad in 1926 for the U.S. School of Music which went on to become one of the most popular and successful ads in history.

As well as rising to the rank of Navy commander during World War II; John wrote several books, including “Tested Advertising Methods” which was first published in 1932 and is now in its 5th edition; and was elected to the American Advertising Federation’s Hall of Fame.

John knew how to write words that sell.

We follow John’s scientific process on writing, and like John, we get results.

Now, we know you’ve struggled to find copywriters who have that special touch when it comes to engaging a reader. (I should know, I’ve been hiring copywriters for over 5 years.)

We understand that struggle, and so we developed a writing process that was measurable and repeatable, and packaged our services up into plans with defined deliverables.

This way you always know what you’re getting and for how much.
